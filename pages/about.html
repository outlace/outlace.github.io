About

This is a machine learning, data science, and anything quantitative/computational blog. Posts are meant to be expository and are generally embedded Jupyter notebooks. Python is the primary programming language used. The blog began as a single-contributor personal blog but I'm planning to transition it into a multi-contributor blog.
